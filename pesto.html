Fresh Pesto

Ingredients

  • ~ 50g Fresh basil leaves
  • 0.5 C Extra Virgin Olive Oil/li>
  • 0.5 C fresh grated Parmesan
  • 2 Tbsp fresh grated Pecorino
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 Tbsp Pine nuts
  • Salt
  • Squirt of Lemon (optional)

Directions

  1. Place cleaned/dry basil in food processor with garlic, lemon, pine nuts(toasted or not), and grated cheeses.
  2. Lightly chop mixture and slowly pour in olive oil while mincing as minimally as possible.
  3. If too thick, add 1 Tbsp of pasta water at a time.
