Homemade Flour Tortillas

Ingredients

  • 300g All Purpose Flour
  • 4g Kosher Salt (1 tsp)
  • 2g (1/2 tsp) Baking Powder

  • 180 mL Hot Water (3/4 C)
  • 60 mL Vegetable Oil (1/4 C)

Directions

  1. Combe Flour, Salt, and Baking Powder in a bowl
  2. Add 180mL Hot water immediately followed by 60 mL of vegetable oil and combine w/ fork or spoon
  3. Roll out onto counter and knead (1-5 mins) under combined and smooth
  4. Allow to rest as a happy ball of dough for 1-2 hours covered in plastic wrap

  5. Heat cast iron pan on MED-HIGH
  6. Divide rested dough into 4-6 balls.
  7. Flour work surface and flatten ball, then roll completely flat w/ rolling pin.
  8. Cook each side in [MED - HIGH] heat cast iron pan for 15-30 seconds.
