Tzatziki Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Plain Greek Yogurt
  • Fresh lemon juice (0.5-1 lemon)
  • 2 Cloves pressed or minced garlic
  • 3 Tbsp fresh chopped dill
  • 3 Tbsp fresh chopped mint leaves
  • Pinch of sea salt and black pepper
  • Half of a large english cucumber - diced
  • Half of a large english cucumber - grated and press of juice

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients and place in fridge covered up to 3 days.
