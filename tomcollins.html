Ingredients

  • 2 fl oz Gin
  • 3/4 fl oz fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 fl oz simple syrup
  • 2 fl oz club soda
  • [optional] Lemon wedge and mint sprig for garnish

Directions

  1. Fill a collins glass with ice and put in freezer while mixing cocktail.
  2. Combine Gin, Lmeon juice, simple syrup and ice in shaker and shake until chilled.
  3. Strain in to emptied collins glass and garnish.
