Authentic Naan (Non-Sourdough)

Ingredients

  • 1/2 C Plain Greek Yogurt
  • 2.5 C Flour
  • 1/2 C Warm Water
  • 1 tsp Sugar
  • 2.5 tsp insant yeast
  • Pinch of Salt
  • 1 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 Tbsp Freshly Pressed Garlic
  • 2 Tbsp Chopped Parsley
  • 3Tbsp unsalted butter

Directions

  1. Add yeast, sugar, salt, and warm water to a bowl - allow to activate yeast ~10mins
  2. Transfer yeast to a large bowl (with flour) and knead until dough becomes tacky and smooth
  3. Cover and allow dough to proof, about 1 hour.
  4. Divide into 8 portions, and cook on cast iron skillet [MED-HIGH]
  5. Melt butter and add garlic + parsley to brush over finish naan.
