Crispy Oven-Baked Chicken Wings

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken wings
  • 1 Tbsp flour
  • 1.5 Tbsp Baking Powder
  • 4 Tbsp spice mix (Ex: Salt/Pepper/GarlicPwdr/etc)

Directions

  1. Place wings on oven racks above a pan lined with aluminum foil.
  2. Baked in oven at 250 F for 30 minutes.
  3. Increase heat to 420F and bake for 40 mins.
  4. Lather in favorite sauce.
