Sourdough Pizza Crust

  • 100 g Sourdough Starter
  • 30 g (2 Tbsp) Olive Oil
  • 10 g Fine Sea Salt
  • 450-500 g all purpose flour
  • 350 g Water

The Night Before (or morning of if low on time):

  1. Add all ingredients and mix/knead dough.
  2. Leave dough at room temperature overnight to proof.
  3. ** If low on time, add fast-acting yeast and complete next morning steps along with these.

The Next Morning

  1. Perform a set of stretch and folds, form into a tight ball of dough and place in the fridge (Up to 36 hours) until ready to use.

When Ready to Use

  1. Remove from dough, rest 30 mins and bring to R.T.
  2. Preheat Broiler - HIGH
  3. Divide into 4 servings, form into balls, and cover/rest 30 mins
  4. Form dough into pizzas, add into hot cast iron pan, and place on sauce + toppings.
  5. Cook until crust is firm, transfer to broiler [HIGH] for 2-4 mins
