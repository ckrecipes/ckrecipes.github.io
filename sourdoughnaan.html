Sourdough Naan

Ingredients

  • 1/4 C (60g) Plain Greek Yogurt
  • 2.5 C (300g) Flour
  • 1/2 C (120g) Milk
  • Pinch of Sugar
  • 1/2 C (125g) Sourdough Discard Starter
  • Pinch of Salt (7g)
  • 1 Tbsp Olive Oil (15g)
  • 1 Tbsp Freshly Pressed Garlic
  • 2 Tbsp Chopped Parsley
  • 3Tbsp unsalted butter

Directions

  1. Mix the ingredients together and form a ball, knead until smooth
  2. Place in a bowl - covered- and allow to proof ~8 hours at RT
  3. Divide into 8 equal portions, and cook on [MED-HIGH] in cast iron skillets
  4. Melt butter and add the garlic and parsley, then brush finish naans
