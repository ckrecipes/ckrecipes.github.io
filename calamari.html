Fried Calamari

Ingredients

  • 1-2 lbs fresh squid, cleaned and cut.
  • 1 cup Half/Half or Heavy Cream
  • 1 cup of italian or panko bread crumbs
  • 1.5 Tbsp Baking Powder
  • 1-2 Tbsp Garlic Powder, Onion Powder, Black Pepper, Salt
  • 1 Tbsp Cayenne Pepper
  • 2 Tbsp freshly chopped parsley

Directions

  1. Clean and thoroughly dry squid and cut into pieces.
  2. Place in bowl with Half/Half or Heavy cream, allow to sit 30 mins
  3. Toss in mixture of breads crumbs, baking powder, and seasonings.
  4. Cook in hot oil (375 F) for ~ 1 min each side.
  5. Strain, spritz with fresh lemon juice and top with parsley.
