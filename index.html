Crispy Oven-Baked Chicken-Wings

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs Chicken Wings(Cut)
  • 0.5 Tbsp Flour
  • 1.5 Tbsp Baking Powder
  • 3-4 Tbsp of Seasoning Mix (Salt/Pepper/Garlic+Onion Powder/etc)

Directions

  1. Evenly coat wings in mixture of Baking powder, Seasoning, and Flour.
  2. Place wings on top of racks on foil-lined pans and bake at 350F for 30 mins to render.
  3. After 30 minutes, increase temperature to 425F and bake 40-45mins to crisp.
  4. Remove from oven and toss desired sauce. See sauces link or click here for GARLICPARMESON or HOTSAUCE

Garlic Parmesan Wing Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 0.5 C Unsalted butter
  • 0.5 Grated Parmesan
  • 1 Tsp Garlic Powder
  • 0.5 Tsp Salt
  • 0.5 Tsp Black Pepper

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients (into warm butter)
  2. Toss wings with warm sauce.
  3. After 30 minutes, increase temperature to 425F and bake 40-45mins to crisp.

Hot Wing Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 1C Frank's Hot Sauce
  • 0.5 Brown Sugar
  • 1/3 C Butter
  • 2 Tbsp Crushed Red Pepper
  • 2 Tbsp Sriracha

Directions

  1. Combine butter and brown sugar in sauce pan and cook until brown sugar is dissolved.
  2. Remove from heat and toss wings
