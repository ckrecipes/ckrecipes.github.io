LOADED Nachos

Ingredients

  • 1 lb seasoned + cooked Grond Beef
  • 1-2 Large bags of tortilla chips
  • ~1 lb Roma Tomatos
  • 0.5 head of Iceburg lettuce (finely chopped)
  • Shredded and/or Queso cheese
  • 0.5-1 C sour cream
  • Fresh Cilantro (chopped)
  • Jalapeno Slices

Directions

  1. Preheat Oven to 225 F
  2. Season, cook, and drain ground beef.
  3. Place tortilla chips topped with ground beef in a foil-lined baking sheet
  4. Add lettuce, tomato, queso, sour cream, cilantro, and jalapeno.
  5. Heat in oven at 225 F for 5-10mins and serve
