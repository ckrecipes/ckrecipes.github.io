French Steamed Mussels

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 1 onion (or shallots) finely chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves finely chopped
  • 2 pounds 1kg mussels, cleaned
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter cut into pieces
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley chopped
  • Salt and cracked black pepper to season
  • 1 tablespoon fresh squeezed lemon juice

Directions

  1. In a bowl, combine chicken with all of the ingredients for the chicken marinade; let marinate for 30 minutes to an hour (or overnight if time allows).
  2. Heat the olive oil and butter in a large pot over medium-high heat. Sauté the onion and garlic until softened, (about 5 minutes).
  3. Add the mussels, wine, cream, butter, and parsley. Season well with salt and pepper, to taste.
