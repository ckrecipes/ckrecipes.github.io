Lemon Drop Martini

Ingredients

  • 1.5 fl oz Citron Vodka
  • 1/2 fl oz Tripel Sec
  • 1/2 fl oz Fresh Lemon Juice
  • 1 tsp Simple Syrup
  • Sugar
  • Lemon Wedge/Twist

Directions

  1. Fill a martini glass with ice and put in freezer while mixing cocktail.
  2. Combine Vodka, Tripel Sec, Lemon Juice, and simple syrup in a shaker and shake until airy and chilled
  3. Remove glass from freezer rub rim with lemon then press into sugar for salted rim
  4. Strain shaker into cocktail glass, add lemon wedge for garnish
